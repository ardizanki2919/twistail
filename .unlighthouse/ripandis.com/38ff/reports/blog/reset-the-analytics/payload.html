I got an issue with my self-hosted Plausible analytics. I'm running Plausible analytics with Docker on a small VM. Unfortunately, the storage needed to be upgraded and this resulted in the Docker instance not running. Unfortunately, when upgrading storage, it turns out that it is not seamless and smooth. I decided to move the VM to another provider and the data on my old VM can't get back up.

Fortunately, I can still make screenshots for my website statistics and these are the latest metrics for my website for the period from April 2022 to April 2023.

My advice: Be aware of your data, and always back up regularly.
